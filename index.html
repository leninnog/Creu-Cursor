Custom Créu Cursor!

Trail cursors, yes, they existed in the old Internet! Could they be coming back? Actually, you can have this one on your own site! Just copy the text inside the box below, paste it between your page's <head> tags and you're done! 
<script src="https://dzshn.github.io/Creu-Cursor/creu-cursor.js" type="text/javascript"></script>

How does it work?

it's simple: the code generates the image objects, and whenever you move the mouse, the code makes the images slowly follow it while also moving the Créu image.
